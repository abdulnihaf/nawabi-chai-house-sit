On estimated Rs 3L/month UPI volume, that's Rs 7,080/month (Rs 85K/year) in payment processing fees that could be Rs 0 with a direct bank QR. Recommendation: Set up direct HDFC bank QR for counter to eliminate this cost.

RUNNER MODEL — NCH's Unique Sales Channel

How the Runner Model Works

NCH has 5 runners who sell chai and snacks at external stations/locations (offices, colleges, markets). Each runner carries stock from the main kitchen, has their own Razorpay QR code for UPI payments, and collects cash. At end of shift, runners settle back to the counter — handing over cash and reconciling UPI collections via the Razorpay dashboard.

Runner Settlement Formula

cashToCollect = (tokensSold + salesTotal) - upiAmount

Each runner must hand over: total value of goods sold (including any tokens) minus UPI already collected digitally. The remainder is physical cash that must be in the runner's pocket. Any variance = leakage or error.

TOKEN SYSTEM — Pre-Paid Chai Tokens

How Tokens Work

Customer pays upfront at counter for N tokens. Each token is redeemable for a specific item (usually cutting chai). Revenue is recognized at time of issue (NCHTI journal). Tokens are a liability until redeemed — but in practice, unredeemed tokens ("breakage") are free revenue.

StepWhat HappensPaymentOdoo JournalFee
1. Token Issued Customer buys tokens at counter (e.g., 10 chai tokens for Rs 200) Cash or UPI NCHTI (NCH Token Issue, ID: 48) 0% (cash) / 2.36% (UPI)
2. Token Redeemed Customer presents token → receives chai Token (pre-paid) NCHTI offset 0% (already paid)

Token breakage = free revenue

Industry average: 5-15% of tokens are never redeemed. For NCH, if monthly token issue is Rs 30,000, unredeemed tokens could be Rs 1,500-4,500/month in pure profit with zero COGS.

NCH vs HE: The Razorpay Tax

HE's Captain UPI (HC01) goes to a direct bank QR — 0% fee. NCH has no direct bank QR — ALL UPI goes through Razorpay at 2.36%. On an Rs 80 order, that's Rs 1.89 lost. Over 100 UPI orders/day, that's Rs 5,670/month or Rs 68K/year that NCH pays and HE doesn't. Priority action: set up direct HDFC bank QR for NCH counter.
